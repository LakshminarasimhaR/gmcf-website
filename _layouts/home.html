Who are we?

Specializing in ex situ conservation and wildlife monitoring, GaiaMitra Collective Foundation (GMCF) applies cutting-edge science and rigorous data analysis to inform effective conservation strategies - both within human-managed environments and in the wild. We are committed to facilitating long-term ecological research at our sites, ensuring that our efforts contribute to lasting, science-based solutions.

Domain areas

  • Zoo Science – Advancing best practices in animal management, welfare, and conservation breeding programs.
  • Capacity Building – Equipping professionals, students, and stakeholders with skills to effectively manage and conserve wildlife.
  • Long-term Ecological Monitoring – Implementing research programs that track biodiversity, inform policies, and guide strategic interventions.
